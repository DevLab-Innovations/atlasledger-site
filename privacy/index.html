Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

The short version: All your financial data stays on your device by default. We collect nothing. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. Optional iCloud sync and accountant sharing are opt-in and encrypted.

Data Collection

Information You Provide

Atlas Ledger is a local-first application. All data is stored on your device by default. The only exceptions are optional iCloud sync (encrypted, user-controlled) and optional accountant share links (zero-knowledge encrypted). Data you have not explicitly chosen to share is never transmitted to external servers:

  • Expense Records: Amount, merchant, category, date, notes, payment method
  • Receipt Images: Photos captured or selected from your photo library
  • Mileage Tracking: Trip start/end locations, distance, date, purpose
  • Income Records: Amount, source, category, date, recurring frequency
  • Bill Records: Bill name, amount, due date, category, payment history
  • Account Information: Account names, balances (all stored locally)
  • Savings Goals: Goal names, target amounts, contributions
  • Voice Memos: Audio recordings attached to expenses, stored as .m4a files locally
  • Speech Transcripts: Text transcribed from voice dictation, stored with the associated expense
  • Pay Schedule: Pay frequency and anchor date used for budget planning
  • AI Correction Feedback: Local corrections to on-device AI suggestions (never synced)
  • Attached Documents: PDFs and images stored in the app's local documents directory, encrypted using iOS Data Protection

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your usage or analytics
  • We do not have access to your financial data
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs

Data Protection & Security

Encryption at Rest

All financial data in Atlas Ledger is encrypted at rest using iOS Data Protection. The protection level is NSFileProtectionCompleteUntilFirstUserAuthentication — iOS hardware encryption applied to all app files. Encryption keys are derived from your device passcode and the device's Secure Enclave.

This protects: the SQLite database, receipt images, mileage history, income records, bill records, account balances, voice memos, speech transcripts, pay schedule data, and all attached documents.

Subscription Status (iOS Keychain)

Your Pro subscription status (tier and expiration date) is cached in the iOS Keychain — a secure, encrypted storage managed by iOS, inaccessible to other apps. Atlas Ledger stores only the resulting tier status; your payment card details are handled entirely by Apple and never reach the app.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All data is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: Encrypted expense, mileage, account, income, bill, pay schedule, and transcript data
  • File System: Encrypted receipt images, voice memo recordings (.m4a), and attached documents
  • iOS Keychain: Pro subscription tier and expiration date (encrypted by iOS)
  • AsyncStorage: On-device AI correction feedback — stored locally only, never synced
  • App Cache Directory: Core ML model files — deletable by clearing app cache

iCloud Sync (Optional)

Atlas Ledger supports optional iCloud sync, available to all users at no cost. When enabled in Settings → iCloud Sync, your data is synced via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. Sync is opt-in and can be disabled at any time.

Accountant Sharing (Optional)

When you use the Share feature to send a report to your accountant:

  • A snapshot of the selected data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before any data leaves the app
  • The encrypted snapshot is uploaded to atlasledger.live solely to host it for the recipient
  • The decryption key is embedded in the share link and is never transmitted to our server (zero-knowledge architecture)
  • Share links expire after the duration you choose (default: 7 days) and can be revoked at any time

If you do not use the Share feature, no data is ever uploaded to atlasledger.live.

Permissions

Camera & Photo Library (Optional)

Used to capture or select receipt photos. Photos are stored locally and encrypted. Never uploaded to external servers.

Microphone (Optional)

Used to record voice memos attached to expenses. Recordings are saved as encrypted .m4a files locally, capped at 2 minutes, and never transmitted.

Speech Recognition (Optional)

Uses Apple's on-device speech recognizer (SFSpeechRecognizer) in on-device mode — no audio is sent to Apple's servers. Transcripts are stored locally.

Location (Optional — Premium Only)

Used for automatic mileage tracking during trips. Location data is stored locally and encrypted. Background location is used only when actively tracking a trip.

On-Device AI Features

Atlas Ledger uses on-device Core ML models for receipt field extraction, voice commands, and natural language queries. No receipt images, voice audio, query text, or expense data is transmitted to any server for ML processing. All inference runs entirely on your device. These features are optional and can be disabled in Settings → Intelligence.

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit 2

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. Atlas Ledger never receives or stores your payment card details. We receive only the resulting tier status (free or Pro).

FinanceKit (Optional)

When you enable FinanceKit integration, data is requested directly from Apple's FinanceKit API. Imported transactions are stored locally and encrypted.

TestFlight Beta

Crash logs may be sent to Apple for debugging. Crash reports do not contain your expense data.

Children's Privacy

Atlas Ledger is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated via the App Store release notes. The "Last Updated" date will be updated at the top of this document.

  • Delete: Permanently delete expenses, receipts, or all data
  • Control: Manage permissions in iOS Settings
