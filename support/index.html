Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your iPhone in a SQLite database protected by iOS Data Protection (AES-256 encryption). Nothing is sent to external servers unless you explicitly enable iCloud Sync or use the Accountant Sharing feature.

How do I restore a cancelled subscription?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases inside the app. This will restore any active subscriptions tied to your Apple ID without charging you again.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Atlas Ledger and tap Cancel. You can also manage it directly from Atlas Ledger Settings → Manage Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

I switched to a new iPhone — how do I transfer my data?

Use Apple's device transfer process (Quick Start or iCloud Backup/Restore) — this transfers the entire app including your SQLite database. Alternatively, enable iCloud Sync in Settings before switching; your data will sync to the new device automatically.

Does receipt scanning require an internet connection?

No. Receipt scanning uses on-device OCR and Core ML models that run entirely offline. No image data is ever sent to a server.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Reports → Export to export your expenses as CSV or PDF. All exports are generated locally on your device.

What is the Accountant Sharing feature?

Accountant Sharing lets you create an encrypted, time-limited link to share a snapshot of your expenses with your accountant or bookkeeper. The data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before upload — our server only holds encrypted ciphertext and never has your decryption key.

The app is asking for Location permission — why?

Location access is optional and only used for GPS live trip tracking (a Premium feature). If you prefer manual mileage entry, you can deny location permission and enter mileage manually. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Atlas Ledger.

How do I delete all my data?

You can delete individual records from within the app. To delete all app data permanently, uninstall Atlas Ledger from your iPhone — this removes the local database and all associated files. If you have iCloud Sync enabled, you can also delete the iCloud data from Settings → iCloud Sync → Delete iCloud Data.

I'm getting a "Cannot restore purchases" error

Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase. Check your internet connection, then try again from Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, contact Apple Support or email us at support@atlas-ledger.com.
