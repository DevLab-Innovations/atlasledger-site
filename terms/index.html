2. Description of Service

Atlas Ledger is a local-first personal finance and expense tracking application for iOS devices. The App provides:

  • Free Features: Single account, expense tracking, receipt scanning (OCR), basic income entry, manual bill tracking, basic categorization, mileage tracking, Excel export, and 3 AI queries/month
  • Premium Features ($14.99/mo or $149.99/yr): All Free features plus unlimited accounts, GPS live trip tracking, unlimited AI queries, cash flow dashboard, quarterly tax estimates, split transactions, Document Wallet, pay period budgeting, financial projections, tax-ready exports, custom reports, savings goals, and Peer Sharing — with a 30-day free trial for new subscribers
  • Peer Sharing Add-on ($4.99/mo): For Free tier users who want to share accounts with Viewer or Editor roles (included automatically with Premium)

The App operates entirely on your device with no cloud storage or external servers required for core features.
