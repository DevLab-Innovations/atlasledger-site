Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your finances

Built for individuals, freelancers, and small business owners who value privacy.

📷

Receipt Scanning

Snap a photo and let on-device OCR extract the merchant, amount, date, and category automatically. No cloud processing — ever.

🚗

Mileage Tracking

Tap to start a trip and GPS logs your route automatically. Get IRS mileage deduction estimates at tax time.

💰

Income & Expense

Track both sides of your cash flow with categories, recurring entries, and split transactions for detailed reporting.

🧾

Bill Tracking

Never miss a payment. Track upcoming and overdue bills with due-date reminders and payment history.

📊

Spending Charts

Radial bar rings visualize your category spending at a glance. Filter by time period to spot trends.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Optionally sync across your Apple devices for free via iCloud. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple.

🤖

On-Device AI

Core ML models suggest categories and extract receipt data entirely on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

📤

Accountant Sharing

Share encrypted snapshots with your accountant via a link. Zero-knowledge: our server never holds your decryption key.

🗓️

Pay Period Budgeting

Align budgets to your actual pay schedule. See exactly how much is left before your next paycheck.

Privacy by Design

Atlas Ledger was built local-first from day one. Your financial data is too sensitive to live on someone else's server.

🔒 No accounts required 📵 No analytics or tracking 🏠 All data on your device 🔐 AES-256 encryption at rest 🤫 No third-party SDKs 🍎 Payments via Apple only
